
THE EIGHT WORLDS BEYOND THE ICE WALL
— Game Lore & Cosmology —

OVERVIEW
Earth is not a planet drifting freely through space.
It is a sealed realm—encased within a firmament—floating in a cosmic ocean.
Beyond this shell exist other enclosed worlds, each its own ecosystem, each governed by ancient laws.

Long ago, balance reigned between these realms.
That balance was broken.

A shadow organization now siphons resources from these worlds, draining life, memory, emotion, and time itself.
The Ethical Avengers are awakened to restore equilibrium.

THE GREAT STRUCTURE
The cosmos is arranged like the Kabbalistic Tree of Life, eight realms positioned within a divine geometric lattice.
Each world is sealed inside a dome and connected by energetic pathways.
At the center lies the Axis — the point of balance.

THE EIGHT WORLDS

1. AURELION – The Sun Garden
A world of radiant flora and living light.
Resource drained: Vital Energy.
Effect on Earth: Burnout, exhaustion, loss of purpose.
Trial: Reignite the Heart Engine without destroying it.

2. NOKTARA – Realm of Shadow Memory
A twilight world where memories manifest as living shadows.
Resource drained: Collective Memory.
Effect: Historical erasure, rewritten truth.
Trial: Navigate lies that reshape reality.

3. VIRELLA – The Verdant Deep
A jungle-ocean hybrid pulsing with life.
Resource drained: Life Essence.
Effect: Ecological collapse.
Trial: Earn the trust of a sentient biosphere.

4. KHEMRA – The Forge World
A molten realm of living metal and sentient machines.
Resource drained: Creative Fire.
Effect: Stagnation, loss of innovation.
Trial: Outcreate an ancient machine god.

5. AETHERION – The Sky Archive
Floating libraries of living thought.
Resource drained: Knowledge.
Effect: Forgetfulness, loss of wisdom.
Trial: Survive knowledge that defends itself.

6. THALASSA – The Deep Mirror
A sentient ocean world reflecting inner truth.
Resource drained: Empathy.
Effect: Emotional numbness.
Trial: Face one’s true self beneath the waves.

7. UMBRA PRIME – The Shadow Engine
A world built from stolen matter.
Resource drained: All others.
Effect: Systemic decay.
Trial: Disrupt the engine without collapsing reality.

8. TERRA PRIMA – The Origin World
The forgotten twin of Earth.
Source of balance.
Final Choice: Restore the cosmic order—or reshape it entirely.

THE ETHICAL AVENGERS
Chosen not by blood but by alignment.
Each resonates with a cosmic principle:
Truth, Balance, Memory, Fire, Will, Time, Compassion, Return.

Their mission is not conquest—but restoration.

END NOTE
When all worlds are healed, the firmament dissolves.
Humanity awakens.
The cosmos breathes again.
