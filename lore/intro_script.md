# Ethical Avengers - 90s Anime Intro Script (2 Minutes)

**Style Guidelines:**
- **Visuals:** 90s OVA Aesthetic (Ninja Scroll / Akira Toriyama). High-contrast shading, film-noir lighting, deep shadows (Chiaroscuro), and clean cel-shaded lines.
- **Cinematography:** One-point perspective, wide-angle shots, dynamic camera movement (Akira style pans).
- **Character - Sophia**: A radiant dark-skinned black woman with long, thin dreadlocks that flow like silver-white liquid light. She wears pristine white garments with intricate gold detailing and has glowing golden cosmic symbols etched into her dark-toned arms.
- **Audio:** Synth-driven 90s orchestral score, visceral sound effects (sword clashes, cosmic hums).

## Sequence Breakdown

### Part 1: The Shattering of Sophia (0:00 - 0:30)
- **Shot 1 (Wide):** The void of the pre-universe. A glowing, ethereal core (Mother Goddess Sophia).
- **Shot 2 (Extreme Close-up):** An evil cosmic shadow (The Organization) reaching with jagged, black tendrils.
- **Shot 3 (Action):** The shadow strikes the core. A silent, blinding explosion of white light.
- **Shot 4 (Montage):** 8 glowing shards of light fly out in different directions. We see them passing through forming nebulas, creating pulsars, and settling into distant planets.
- **Shot 5 (Text Overlay):** "THE SOUL OF THE GODDESS... SHATTERED."

### Part 2: Awakening in Antarctica (0:30 - 1:00)
- **Shot 6 (Extreme Wide):** The vast Antarctic ice walls. A blizzard rages. 
- **Shot 7 (Medium):** High-tech Organization minions drill into a glowing fissure.
- **Shot 8 (Close-up):** A shard pulses.
- **Shot 9 (Action - Stage 1: Demonstration):** The minions fire plasma cannons. Naga Soul appears, **grabs a blast mid-air**, and crushes it like glass. Silent pause, 3-frame impact freeze. 
- **Shot 10 (Action - Stage 2: Environmental Destruction):** Verona Rose (Cherry) launches a photon blitz. The **ice wall behind them splits for miles**. 

### Part 3: The Spiritual Council (1:00 - 1:30)
- **Shot 11 (Ethereal):** The Avengers are pulled into a spirit realm. A council of ancient, celestial beings (translucent and glowing) appears.
- **Shot 12 (Flashback Speed-run):** Rapid-fire montage of cosmic history: The Great War, The Rise of the Organization, The Prophecy of the Shards.
- **Shot 13 (Close-up):** The Council gestures toward a vision of a unified Sophia.
- **Shot 14 (Dialogue/Voiceover):** "YOU ARE THE FINAL DEFENSE. RECLAIM THE SHARDS. REVIVE THE MOTHER."

### Part 4: The Final Conflict (1:30 - 2:00)
- **Shot 15 (Action - Stage 3: Cosmic Abilities):** Space battle. Dr. Jack opens a temporal rift; we see **two Dr. Jacks** landing perfect tactical strikes from multiple timelines.
- **Shot 16 (Action - Stage 4: Mythic Power):** Big Zeeko slams his fists. **Gravity flips**. Fallen enemies rise toward the mothership like meteorites.
- **Shot 17 (The 'Oh Sh*t' Moment):** The Organization Boss absorbs a Shard. The sky tears open into an aurora spiral. He sneers: *"The age of gods is over."*
- **Shot 18 (Stinger):** Naga Soul, surrounded by a cosmic serpentine aura (Astrality Coil), replies: *"Then why are you shaking?"*
- **Shot 19 (Title Card):** **ETHICAL AVENGERS** (Heavy camera shake, impact pause).
