# Ethical Avengers - Cinematic Shot List (10/10 Action Framework)

This document outlines the specific visual composition and technical requirements for each shot in the intro sequence, following the **Cass Action Framework**.

## Legend
- **Impact Pause**: A momentary freeze (3-5 frames) on the moment of contact to sell power.
- **Speed Lines**: High-velocity streaks in the background to emphasize movement.
- **Environmental Reaction**: Debris, craters, or environmental changes resulting from player actions.

---

## Act 1: The Shattering
**Focus: Scale & Mystery**

- **Shot 1 (Extreme Wide):** The Mother Core Sophia in the center of the void. Soft, liquid-light dreadlocks pulsing.
- **Shot 2 (POV Shadow):** The jagged black tendrils of The Organization closing in. One-point perspective.
- **Shot 3 (Impact):** The core is struck. **White Flash → Impact Pause.** The screen shatters like glass. 
- **Shot 4 (Cinematic Pan):** 8 Shards streaking through the cosmos. Deep space nebulas in the background.

---

## Act 2: Demonstration & Destruction (Antarctica)
**Focus: Identifying the Heroes & The Power Gap**

- **Shot 5 (Wide):** The Ethical Avengers silhouetted against a massive blizzard on the ice wall.
- **Shot 6 (Medium - Naga Soul):** Naga Soul stands casually. An Organization projectile enters the frame.
- **Shot 7 (Close-up - Stage 1):** Naga Soul catches the blast mid-air. **Impact Pause.** He crushes it. Glow reflection on his visor.
- **Shot 8 (Action - Stage 2):** Verona Rose (Cherry) leaps. Wide shot. She kicks toward the glacier.
- **Shot 8b (Fight Sequence):** Detailed Shintani-style fight script and AI prompts located in `STORYBOARD-INTRO/intro_anime_fight_storyboard.md`
- **Shot 9 (Environmental Reaction):** The point of impact on the glacier. **Impact Pause.** A massive crack splits the ice wall for miles. Glacial collapse.

---

## Act 3: Cosmic Abilities (The Shift)
**Focus: Bending Physics & Tactical Prowess**

- **Shot 10 (Spirit Realm):** Dr. Jack in a grid-like HUD space. Time is slowed.
- **Shot 11 (Cosmic - Dr. Jack):** Multiple Dr. Jacks appearing from temporal rifts, landing strikes simultaneously on a giant mech.
- **Shot 12 (Signature Silhouette):** Naga Soul's **Cosmic Overdrive**. A pure, violently bright cosmic aura erupts around him as he drops into a martial arts stance, shattering the ground beneath him.

---

## Act 4: Mythic Power & The Boss
**Focus: God-tier Scale & The "Oh Sh*t" Moment**

- **Shot 13 (Space Wide):** The team in orbit against the Organization Mothership.
- **Shot 14 (Mythic - Big Zeeko):** Zeeko (Super Strength/Gravity Specialist) slams his fists on a metal hull piece. **Gravity Flips.** Debris and enemies fall *upward* toward the stars.
- **Shot 15 (The Boss):** The Organization Leader absorbs a Shard. Sky tears open into a pulsing spiral aurora.
- **Shot 16 (Stinger Close-up):** Naga Soul replies: *"Then why are you shaking?"* Intense cosmic energy flaring from his eyes in the shadow of his gas mask.
- **Shot 17 (Title Reveal):** **ETHICAL AVENGERS** slams onto screen. **Heavy Camera Shake.** Screen fades to white.

---

## Production Notes
- **Scaling**: All characters must maintain absolute fidelity to the 10/10 master portraits.
- **Framing**: Use Stanley Kubrick-inspired symmetry for non-action shots to emphasize order vs. chaos.
- **Color**: Heavy use of cyan/magenta neon accents against dark, high-contrast backgrounds.
