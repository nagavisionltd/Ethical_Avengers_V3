# THE EIGHT WORLDS BEYOND THE ICE WALL
— Official Game Lore & Cosmology —

## OVERVIEW
Earth is not a planet drifting freely through space. It is a sealed realm—encased within a firmament—floating in a cosmic ocean. Beyond this shell exist other enclosed worlds, each its own ecosystem, each governed by ancient laws.

Long ago, balance reigned between these realms. That balance was broken when a Shadow Organization began siphoning the lifeblood of the cosmos: the shards of the Goddess Sophia's soul.

## THE CORE MISSION
The Goddess **SOPHIA** (Goddess of Truth and Balance) has been shattered. Her essence has been distilled into **Pulsating Origin Gems (P.O.G.)** and scattered across the Eight Worlds. The Ethical Avengers are awakened to retrieve these shards, outcreate the machine gods, and restore Sophia to her full power to dissolve the Firmament.

## THE EIGHT WORLDS

1. **AURELION** (The Sun Garden): Radiant flora and living light. Siphoned: Vital Energy.
2. **NOKTARA** (Realm of Shadow Memory): Monochrome city of shadows. Siphoned: Collective Memory.
3. **VIRELLA** (The Verdant Deep): Jungle-ocean hybrid. Siphoned: Life Essence.
4. **KHEMRA** (The Forge World): Molten realm of sentient metal. Siphoned: Creative Fire.
5. **AETHERION** (The Sky Archive): Floating thought libraries. Siphoned: Knowledge.
6. **THALASSA** (The Deep Mirror): Sentient ocean reflecting fears. Siphoned: Empathy.
7. **UMBRA PRIME** (The Shadow Engine):Dystopian fortress of stolen matter. Headquarters of the Shadow Org.
8. **TERRA PRIMA** (The Origin World): The twin of Earth. The battery of the cosmos.

## END GOAL
When all shards (P.O.G.) are returned to Sophia, the Firmament dissolves. Humanity awakens from its slumber. The cosmos breathes again.
