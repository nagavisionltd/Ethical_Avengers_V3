# The Eight Worlds: 9-Stage Storyline Mapping

This document maps the mission of the Ethical Avengers: **Lord Soul**, **Leon G**, **Verona Rose**, **Big Zeeko**, and **Dr. Jack**, guided by the Goddess **Sophia**.

---

## 🌎 STAGE 1: The Antarctic Perimeter (The Ice Wall)
**Theme:** The Breach. Breaching the firmament to find the first shard of Sophia's soul.
**Guardian:** The Shadow Guard.
**Naga Soul:** "Protection looks a lot like a cage. We’re breaking out."

---

## ☀️ STAGE 2: Aurelion (The Sun Garden)
**Theme:** Radiance. Collecting the Shard of Vitality.
**Mission:** Reignite the Heart Engine. Unplug the harvester ships bottling the sunlight.

---

## 🌑 STAGE 3: Noktara (Realm of Shadow Memory)
**Theme:** Truth. Collecting the Shard of Memory.
**Mission:** Navigate a monochrome cityscape where shadows detach from reality. Retrieve the history stolen from humanity.

---

## 🌿 STAGE 4: Virella (The Verdant Deep)
**Theme:** Growth. Collecting the Shard of Life.
**Mission:** Heal the calcifying biosphere. Show the sentient planet that the Avengers are the cure, not the virus.

---

## ⚙️ STAGE 5: Khemra (The Forge World)
**Theme:** Innovation. Collecting the Shard of Creation.
**Mission:** Outcreate the Ancient Machine God. Short-circuit the industrial brutalist factories of the Shadow Org.

---

## 🏛️ STAGE 6: Aetherion (The Sky Archive)
**Theme:** Wisdom. Collecting the Shard of Knowledge.
**Mission:** Infiltrate floating libraries. Reclaim the wisdom locked away in armored server-vaults.

---

## 🌊 STAGE 7: Thalassa (The Deep Mirror)
**Theme:** Empathy. Collecting the Shard of Truth.
**Mission:** Face your inner mirror in a world where the sky is the ocean. Defeat your own darkest reflections.

---

## ⬛ STAGE 8: Umbra Prime (The Shadow Engine)
**Theme:** The Reckoning. Reclaiming all siphoned power.
**Mission:** Infiltrate the Shadow Organization's headquarters. Disrupt the engine built from stolen worlds without collapsing reality.

---

## 🌌 STAGE 9: Terra Prima (The Origin World)
**Theme:** The Return. Restoration of the cosmic order.
**Finale:** Face the Grand Architect in the core of the cosmos. Return all shards to Sophia to restore her soul and dissolve the Firmament.

---

## 🏆 THE ENDING
*   **Restoration:** Sophia is restored. The Firmament dissolves. Earth ascends to its true place among the 8 worlds.
