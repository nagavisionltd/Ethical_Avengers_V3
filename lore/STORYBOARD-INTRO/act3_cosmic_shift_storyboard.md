# Intro Cinematic: Act 3 - The Cosmic Shift
**Format:** Anime Action Storyboard (Shintani-Style Fluidity)
**Setting:** A distorted, grid-like spiritual realm transitioning into a high-orbit space battle.

## 1. Combat Matchup Map
*   **The Threat:** The Organization's "Chronos Void" (Disruptor Type). A shifting, amorphous mass of dark matter that absorbs conventional energy attacks.
*   **Dr. Jack (Disruptor - Scissors):** Wearing his signature glasses, his aura is a geometric, grid-like digital field. He manipulates the "flow" of combat through temporal rifts.
*   **Naga Soul (Armament/Cosmic - Rock):** Pure cosmic energy erupting from a grounded martial arts stance. He is the heavy hitter who shatters the void's core.

---

## 2. The Sequence Script (Temporal Prowess & High-Impact Arts)
The screen shifts into a digital, grid-like HUD. **Dr. Jack** stands in the center of the Spirit Realm. Time slows to a crawl. The *Chronos Void* lunges, its tendrils expanding into massive obsidian blades. 

Dr. Jack doesn't move. He simply adjusts his glasses. Geometric "temporal rifts" open around the room like shattered mirrors. From each rift, a different iteration of Dr. Jack steps out simultaneously. One strikes the Void's flank, another lands a spinning kick to its center, another fires a digital Disruptor pulse. **Shintani-style motion smears** connect these multiple Dr. Jacks into a flurry of overwhelming tactical strikes. 

As the *Chronos Void* is forced into its core containment form to survive the barrage, **Naga Soul** enters the frame. He drops into a low, centered martial arts stance. No weapons. Noสัตว์. Just raw cosmic power. 

His aura erupts in a violently bright, blinding pillar of white-blue energy (**Cosmic Overdrive**). The ground of the digital realm shatters into floating geometric shards. He drives a single, concentrated dragon-punch directly into the center of the dark matter mass. **Impact Pause (5 frames).** 

---

## 3. The Silent Climax (Vacuum Effect)
The moment Naga Soul's fist connects, the digital grid dissolves. **Audio cuts to absolute zero.** 
The camera zooms in on Naga Soul's eyes behind the gas mask—they are twin suns of pure cosmic intensity. The *Chronos Void* isn't just hit; it’s being erased from the inside out by the purity of the strike.

---

## 4. The Resulting Transition
The audio returns with an earth-shaking *BOOM.* The "camera" lens cracks from the sheer kinetic force.
Instead of a normal explosion, the void shatters into 8 glowing **Mother Core Shards** that streak across the sky, transitioning the scene from the Spirit Realm into a wide-angle shot of the team in high-orbit, orbit against the Organization Mothership.
