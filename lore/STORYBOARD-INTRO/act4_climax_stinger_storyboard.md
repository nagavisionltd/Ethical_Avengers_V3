# Intro Cinematic: Act 4 - The Climax & Stinger
**Format:** Anime Action Storyboard (Shintani-Style Fluidity)
**Setting:** High-orbit above Earth, the atmosphere shimmering with aurora-like energy.

## 1. Combat Matchup Map
*   **The Threat:** The Organization Mothership & The Leader. A titan-class vessel protected by a Shard-powered aurora shield.
*   **Big Zeeko (Armament/Cosmic - Rock):** A tactical combatant with super strength and gravity-manipulation abilities. He provides the raw power required to breach the hull.
*   **The Team (Joint Assault):** Leon G, Cherry, and Naga Soul combining their powers for the final breach.

---

## 2. The Sequence Script (The Mythic Breach)
The team streaks through the upper atmosphere as 8 glowing shards. They converge on the massive Organization Mothership. 

**Big Zeeko** slams his fists onto a detached piece of the hull. **Impact Pause.** Suddenly, gravity flips. Debris, enemy drones, and loose plating fall *upward* away from the ship and into the void of space. Zeeko’s Armament aura turns a deep, crushing purple.

The **Organization Leader** appears on the command deck, absorbing a Shard into a custom gauntlet. The sky above the ship tears open into a pulsing, spiral aurora.

**Naga Soul** (Cosmic Overdrive), **Leon G** (Golden Shield), and **Cherry** (Emissive Ribbon) combine their auras into a single piercing spear of light. They fly directly into the aurora's center. 

---

## 3. The Stinger (The Iconic Line)
The screen goes black for a fraction of a second.
The **Organization Leader** sneers from the shadows: *"You're too late. The system is already online."*
Close-up on **Naga Soul's** gas mask. His eyes flare with an intense, steady cosmic glow. 
Naga Soul replies: *"Then why are you shaking?"*

---

## 4. The Title Slam
**KRAK-THOOM!**
The **ETHICAL AVENGERS** logo slams onto the screen with **Heavy Camera Shake**.
Jagged fissures of energy (Shintani-style) erupt from the lettering, shattering the frame into a blinding white flash.
The game transitions to the **Main Menu/Mode Select**.
