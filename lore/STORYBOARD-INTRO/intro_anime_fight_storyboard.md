# Intro Cinematic: The Antarctica Ice Wall Ambush
**Format:** Anime Action Storyboard (Shintani-Style Fluidity)
**Setting:** A howling blizzard atop the collapsing Antarctican Ice Wall.

## 1. Combat Matchup Map
*   **The Threat:** The Organization's "Glacier Behemoth" Mech (Armament Type). Heavy physical armor, fires devastating condensed-plasma artillery.
*   **Leon G (Armament - Rock):** Yellow armor glowing with a dense, golden kinetic aura. He acts as the immovable shield.
*   **Verona Rose / Cherry (Emissive - Paper):** Pink/Cyan energy streaks. High mobility, zone control via wide spatial kicks.
*   **Naga Soul (Disruptor - Scissors):** Trench coat billowing. A pure cosmic aura, relying on high-impact martial arts strikes that erode tech shielding.

---

## 2. The Sequence Script (Fluidity & Motion)
The blizzard rages. A massive condensed-plasma artillery shell screams through the storm, aimed directly at the team. 
**Leon G** steps forward. His yellow armor flares with a dense, metallic-gold aura. He doesn't brace; he simply swats the artillery shell like a fly. **Impact Pause (3 frames)**. The shell deflects upward, detonating in the clouds, parting the blizzard in a perfect circle above them.

From the snowstorm, the towering Glacier Behemoth Mech charges, the ground cracking under its sheer mass.
**Cherry** is already moving. Motion smears stretch her form into a ribbon of pink and cyan light. She ricochets off Leon's shoulder, launching high above the mech. She unleashes an Emissive aerial barrage—a dozen crescent-shaped energy kicks that rain down, suppressing the Mech's anti-air cannons.

The Mech raises its heavy Vanguard Shield, tanking the energy barrage, but the barrage was just a distraction. 
**Naga Soul** slides across the slick ice underneath the Mech's guard. His gas mask eyes flare. Channeling a blinding cosmic power (Disruptor), he drops into a low martial arts stance and drives a devastating palm strike directly into the Mech's core shielding. His cosmic aura violently short-circuits the armor, stripping away its defense in a chaotic burst of magenta and green sparks.

---

## 3. The Silent Climax (Vacuum Effect)
With the Mech's armor stripped, Leon G closes the gap instantly. 
He pulls back his fist, channeling every ounce of his golden Armament aura into a single focal point.
For one full second, **all audio drops out.** 
No blizzard wind. No mechanical groans. Utter silence. The golden aura around Leon's fist shrinks into an impossibly bright, hyper-condensed pinprick of light. 

---

## 4. The Screen Shatter
Leon's fist connects with the naked chassis of the Mech. 
*KRAK-THOOM!* The audio roars back at 200% volume.
**Impact Pause.** 
The force of the blow doesn't just launch the Mech; it literally cracks the "camera lens." Jagged fissures of light spiderweb across the screen in an X-shaped composition. The sheer kinetic shockwave violently splits the 500-foot ice wall beneath them in half. 
The screen shatters completely into white shards, fading into the **"ETHICAL AVENGERS"** title card.
